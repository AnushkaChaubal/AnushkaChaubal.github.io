About Me

Hello! I'm Anushka Manish Chaubal, a dedicated and enthusiastic Masters of Computer Science student with a strong passion for the field of Data Analysis and Data Science. My areas of expertise lie in Exploratory Data Analysis and Data Visualization, and I thrive on the excitement of unraveling meaningful insights from complex datasets.

Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in data analysis, enabling me to work with diverse industries such as Healthcare, Nutrition, sales, and retail. By delving deep into the intricacies of these domains, I have gained valuable experience in handling data from various sources and understanding the unique challenges each industry presents.

One of my greatest strengths is my ability to uncover hidden patterns, trends, and correlations within data. I have a keen eye for detail and a knack for asking the right questions that lead to valuable discoveries.

I am incredibly passionate about leveraging the power of data to drive informed decision-making and generate actionable insights. With my solid background in exploratory data analysis, data visualization, and experience in various industries, I am ready to take on new challenges and make a significant impact in the world of data analysis and data science
